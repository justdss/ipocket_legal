FAQ

How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to your device Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions (iOS) or Google Play → Subscriptions (Android), find UpMind and tap Cancel. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.
How do I restore my purchase?
Open UpMind, go to the subscription screen and tap Restore Purchase. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account you used when purchasing.
Is my data backed up?
All data (tasks, habits, finance, sport, settings) is stored locally on your device. No data is sent to any server. If you delete the app or reset your device, your data will be lost.
How do I delete my data?
Uninstalling the app removes all local data. If you submitted anything via Contact Us, email us and we will delete it promptly.
The app is not working correctly. What should I do?
Try restarting the app or reinstalling it. If the issue persists, contact us with a description of the problem, your device model, and OS version.

Subscription

UpMind offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions (monthly and yearly) via the App Store or Google Play Store. Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.

To manage or cancel: Apple ID Settings → Subscriptions or Google Play → Subscriptions.

For refund requests, visit reportaproblem.apple.com (iOS) or the Google Play Help Center (Android).

Privacy

No personal data is sent to any server. All app data is stored locally on your device only.
